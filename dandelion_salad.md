# Dandelion, beans and brown rice salad

## Ingredients

- 350g Dandelion leaves
- 1 can of beans
- 1/2 cup brown rice

For the vinaigrette:

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp tahini
- juice and zest of 1 lemon
- 1/2 tsp salt, or to taste
- black pepper, to taste
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp sumac
- 1 garlic clove, grated
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
